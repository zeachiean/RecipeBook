## RecipeBook is a profession recipe browser for TBC Anniversary. Browse every recipe by source, track what you know, filter by zone and phase, and set waypoints to vendors and trainers.

<img src="https://breakbone-addons.com/images/recipebook-screenshot.png" alt="RecipeBook Main Window" width="500"/>

## Features

### Browse All Recipes

View every TBC profession recipe organized by source type — Trainer, Vendor, Quest, Drop, and more. 12 professions supported: Alchemy, Blacksmithing, Cooking, Enchanting, Engineering, First Aid, Fishing, Jewelcrafting, Leatherworking, Mining, Poisons, and Tailoring.

### Source Grouping

Recipes are grouped into collapsible sections by how you obtain them. Expand or collapse Trainer, Vendor, Drop, Quest, and other categories to focus on what matters.

### Known Recipe Tracking

Open your profession window and RecipeBook automatically scans what you've learned. Toggle "Hide Known" to see only the recipes you still need.

### Continent & Zone Filtering

Narrow results by continent and zone. "Auto" mode detects where you are and filters automatically. Instance zones are mapped to their real-world continents.

### Phase Filtering

Filter recipes by content phase (1-5). Only see recipes available in the current phase of the server, or browse ahead to plan your progression.

### Faction Filter

Toggle "My Faction" to hide recipes from the opposite faction's vendors and quests. Non-BoP vendor recipes remain visible since they're tradeable via the neutral Auction House — opposite-faction vendors are marked with (A) or (H). Enabled by default.

### Search

Search across all recipes by name. Results update as you type.

### Waypoint Integration

Click the green arrow next to any vendor or trainer to set a TomTom waypoint via AddressBook. Requires both AddressBook and TomTom addons.

### Recipe Linking

Shift-click any recipe to link it in chat.

## Data Sources

Recipe data is sourced from [RecipeMaster TBC](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/recipe-master).

## Usage

- **Minimap button**: Left-click to open
- `/rb` — Toggle RecipeBook
- `/rb phase <N>` — Set max phase (1-5)
- `/rb reset` — Reset window position

## Installation

Extract the `RecipeBook` folder into your WoW AddOns directory:

```
World of Warcraft/_anniversary_/Interface/AddOns/RecipeBook/
```

After installation, open all of your profession panels to allow RecipeBook to scan your known recipes.

AddressBook and TomTom are optional but recommended for waypoint integration.
